**title Curse of Love & Aliens
*author Atharva Raut 

*create charm 0
*create trust 50
*create courage 10
*create revenge 0
*create power 100

*stat_chart
  percent charm
  percent trust
  percent courage
  percent revenge
  percent power

You are standing in a quiet park. The soft breeze brushes your face. Today might change everything. 

*choice
  #Walk towards Alex.
    *set charm +2
    You walk towards Alex with confidence. His eyes meet yours and he smiles.
    *goto love
  #Ignore Alex and wander alone.
    *set charm -1
    You pretend not to notice him and wander on your own. A pang of loneliness hits you.
    *goto love
